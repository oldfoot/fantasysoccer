STIGMATA
Macintosh & Windows TrueType
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Stigmata genesis -
Based on the the typeface used on the album "Land of Rape & Honey" by Ministry. I just named the font after the first track on the album.

This has absolutely NOTHING to do with religion Ð so all you religious freaks, back off !!!.

Stigmata - The lowdown -
Uppercase, lowercase a smaller version of the uppercase letters, numerals and some punctuation.
